About Intaglios - The Design Society of NSUT

Intaglios - The Design Society of Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT) is a vibrant and dynamic collective of creative minds, passionate about transforming imagination into reality. As NSUT's premier design society, Intaglio serves as a hub for students who thrive on innovation, aesthetics, and storytelling through art and design.
